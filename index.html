Artsy.

where student artists take their first step.

Making potentials noticed.

Artsy allow art students to show off their artworks to their peers. Showing off the effort they put in, students can share their work with all the students in Artsy.

Low cost,
High quality.

Artsy promotes transactions between students. Buying artworks is more accessible while maintaining the quality of the work by only allowing college students to upload their work.

For students,
From students,
With students.

Artsy operates exclusively for students. Artsy allows selling only for student artists and buying for fellow students as well.
